💡 Key Insights

⏱️ Time & Licensing Bottlenecks
Short timelines and BAU load were the most common pain points. Early provisioning of licenses (e.g., Copilot, Gemini) and clearer time commitments to ROs would meaningfully reduce friction.
🧪 Enablers: Clinics + Clear Instructions
AI Clinics, workshops, and clear instructions/resources were repeatedly cited as what worked best — keep and scale these. Sharing tool lists (video editing, narration) would help newcomers ramp faster.
🚀 Strong Continuation Intent
100% of responses are either continuing, open to continue, or unsure — with adoption already at team/beyond-team level in 91% of cases. Focus support on maturing these trajectories.
🎓 Upskilling Needs
Prompt-engineering basics, data/tool access, and targeted technical mentorship are the top support asks. Consider a recurring “Fri learning” cadence and lightweight check-ins.
📣 Evangelism & Feedback Loops
Some teams need marketing/comms and leadership advocacy to drive adoption. Where external tools are involved (e.g., Microsoft), a feedback channel would help teams close the loop.
